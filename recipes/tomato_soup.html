Tomato Soup

This tomato soup is so delicious you may just soil your pants

Probably my favourite dish in the world. Tomato soup for president!

Ingredients

  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken of beef stock
  • Garlic
  • Fresh basil
  1. Heat up a pan, pour some olive oil
  2. Add chopped garlic and some basil, fry for a brief moment, stirring
  3. Add tomato paset, fry on low heat for 15-20 minutes
  4. In a seperate pan, heat up the stock
  5. Add fried paste to the stock, bring to boil
  6. Add basil, simmer for 10 minutes
